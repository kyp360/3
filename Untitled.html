What is IELTS?

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is a standardized test designed to measure English language proficiency for non-native English speakers. It's widely recognized by universities, employers, and immigration authorities worldwide.

IELTS assesses four key language skills: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking, providing a comprehensive evaluation of your English communication abilities.

Academic Training

For university admissions and professional certifications

General Training

For immigration, work experience, and training programs

Global Recognition

Accepted by over 11,000 organizations in 140 countries

Band Scoring

Scores range from 1-9, with most universities requiring 6.5-7.5
